Questions couples ask

Is this a real gift, or just an online thing?
Both. The digital version ($139) gives instant access the moment your guest buys it. The physical gift box ($174) adds a keepsake box with welcome card and notepad. Some couples prefer the box for the unboxing moment.
When should we do the exercises?
Whenever feels right. Some couples do them in the weeks before the wedding to surface expectations. Others wait until a quiet moment after the honeymoon. There's no expiration.
Is this a therapy thing?
No. Attune is a self-guided assessment, not therapy. It surfaces how you each see things and gives you language to talk about them. A structured conversation starter, not a clinical tool.
What if we already own this?
Gift cards are valid indefinitely and transferable. Pass it to another couple, or redeem it for the anniversary package later if the newlywed version doesn't fit.
We're not sure our guests will get it.
The friends and family closest to you already know you're the kind of people who'd want this. Registry guests looking for something meaningful tend to pick Attune first.
