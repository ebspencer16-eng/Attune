I.
What it is

A foundational assessment for couples, designed to be taken once and returned to often.

From the methodology

We assume more than we realize. Putting language to it is the first move.

Attune is two exercises and a shared results experience. Each partner answers independently. The results merge into a single document showing where you align, where you diverge, and where the conversation might go next.

You take it once. You keep it. You return to it as life changes. The questions are written from a clinical foundation, not from intuition or vibes, and the framing avoids the false certainty of personality quizzes. What you get back is a working document, not a verdict.
