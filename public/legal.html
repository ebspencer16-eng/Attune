Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026 · Last updated: April 24, 2026

Pending counsel review. This policy reflects Attune's intended data practices. It has not been reviewed by an attorney. We may revise the text and will update the effective date if we do.

Our core commitment

Attune is built on a simple principle: the most personal things you share with us should never be used against you. We do not sell data. We do not build advertising profiles. Your exercise responses exist for one purpose — to generate insights for you and your partner.

What we collect and how it's separated

Attune stores two categories of data in separate systems, linked only by a randomly generated anonymous identifier. Your name and email are not stored in the system that holds your exercise responses.

  • Identity data — your name and email address. Used for account authentication and to deliver your results to the right account. Stored in our authentication system.
  • Experience data — your exercise responses, results, demographic information, and package selection. Stored under a randomly generated anonymous identifier. If your account is deleted, the identity data is removed and the anonymous experience record can no longer be traced back to you.

Demographic information

During account creation, we ask optional questions about age range, gender, relationship status, length of relationship, number of children, and how you heard about Attune. These questions are optional. Demographic information is stored only in the anonymous experience record — it is not linked to your name or email. It is used in aggregate to understand how Attune serves different types of couples and to improve the product.

How your exercise answers are used

Your responses are used to generate your joint results. Results are only made visible once both partners have completed their exercises independently. Neither partner can see the other's individual responses — only the combined insights. Anonymized responses may be included in aggregate analysis to improve Attune's question design and result interpretation over time.

Order and payment data

Payment details (card numbers, expiration dates, CVC) are handled directly by Stripe, our payment processor. Attune does not receive, store, or transmit payment card information. We retain the package type purchased, the transaction date, the buyer's email, and billing address for tax and accounting purposes, as required by applicable law.

Service providers (subprocessors)

Attune uses the following service providers to operate the product. Each is bound by contractual data-protection obligations. We share only the data each needs to perform its function.

  • Stripe — payment processing and sales tax calculation
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting (United States)
  • Vercel — website and API hosting (United States)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Sentry — error monitoring (receives error messages and stack traces; does not receive your exercise responses)
  • A licensed marriage and family therapist (LMFT) — only for Premium customers who book a session. Anonymized results are shared with the therapist prior to the scheduled session. Your therapist operates under applicable professional confidentiality requirements.

Data sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party.
  • We do not share exercise responses or results with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We may share information when required by law (e.g., valid subpoena, court order) or to protect the rights and safety of our users or others.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you by email before any such transfer occurs.

What we explicitly do not do

  • We do not link your name or email to your exercise responses.
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting.
  • We do not build behavioral profiles linked to your identity.
  • We do not share individual responses — only aggregate, anonymized insights.

Data retention

Your anonymous experience record is retained for as long as your account exists, so you can revisit your results. Your identity data is retained for as long as you have an active account. Order records (for tax and accounting) are retained for seven years as required by applicable law.

You may delete your account at any time from your account settings, or by emailing hello@attune-relationships.com. Upon deletion, identity data is removed within 30 days. Anonymous experience records may be retained in aggregate form but can no longer be linked back to you.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated identity data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing (e.g., email marketing)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@attune-relationships.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, the right to correct it, and the right to opt out of sale or sharing. Attune does not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA. To exercise your California rights, email hello@attune-relationships.com.

International users

Attune operates from the United States and data is stored on U.S.-based servers. If you access Attune from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States. We do not currently target or market Attune to residents of the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland. If you reside in one of these jurisdictions and have questions about your rights, contact us at hello@attune-relationships.com.

Children

Attune is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, email hello@attune-relationships.com.

Cookies and tracking

We use minimal, necessary cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use advertising trackers or cross-site tracking pixels. We use Sentry for error monitoring and may sample a small percentage of sessions for performance analysis — this data is scrubbed of identifying information before transmission.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Identity data and experience data are stored in separate tables with distinct access controls. We follow industry-standard practices for access control, secrets management, and security review. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to us or stored by us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify registered users by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Attune after the notice constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@attune-relationships.com. We respond within two business days.
